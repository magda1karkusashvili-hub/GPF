About Our Federation

Georgian Paragliding Federation develops safe aviation culture, professional pilot training and world-class paragliding standards.

🎯

Mission

The mission of the Georgian Paragliding Federation is to develop paragliding in Georgia as a safe, structured and internationally recognized aviation sport.

The mission of the Georgian Paragliding Federation is to develop paragliding in Georgia as a safe, structured and internationally recognized aviation sport. We aim to provide unforgettable tandem flight experiences while maintaining the highest levels of safety, pilot professionalism, and operational discipline. Our goal is to make every flight not just an adventure, but a fully controlled and trusted aviation experience for both local and international guests.

🛡

Safety

Safety is the core principle of every flight operation.

Safety is the core principle of our organization. We strictly follow international aviation standards and operational procedures aligned with FAI guidelines. All pilots are certified, regularly trained, and continuously assessed. Equipment is inspected before and after every flight, and flights are conducted only under suitable meteorological conditions. Risk management, pre-flight briefing, and emergency preparedness are mandatory parts of every operation.

🎓

Development

Supporting pilot education and the future of paragliding in Georgia.

We actively contribute to the development of paragliding as a professional sport in Georgia. This includes pilot training programs, safety workshops, instructor development, and cooperation with international aviation organizations. We support young pilots and new talents by providing structured learning environments and real flight experience. Our long-term goal is to build a strong aviation community that meets global standards.
