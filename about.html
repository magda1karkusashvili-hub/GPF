About Our Federation

Georgian Paragliding Federation develops safe aviation culture, professional pilot training and world-class paragliding standards.

🎯

Mission

Developing paragliding in Georgia as a safe and internationally recognized aviation sport.

We provide structured training, safe flight operations, professional pilot development and international cooperation.
🛡

Safety

Safety is the core principle of every flight.

All operations follow aviation safety principles, weather assessment, equipment checks and pilot responsibility.
🎓

Development

Supporting future pilots and paragliding growth in Georgia.

We support education, training programs, international standards and aviation culture development.
