About Our Federation

Georgian Paragliding Federation develops safe aviation culture, professional pilot training and international paragliding standards.

🎯 Mission

Develop paragliding in Georgia as a safe, structured and internationally recognized aviation sport.

🛡 Safety

Safety is the core principle of every flight operation.
