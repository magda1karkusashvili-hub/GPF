About Our Federation

Georgian Paragliding Federation develops safe aviation culture, professional pilot training and international paragliding standards.

🎯 Mission

Developing paragliding in Georgia as a safe and internationally recognized aviation sport.

🛡 Safety

Safety is the core principle of every flight. We follow aviation safety principles, weather assessment and pilot responsibility.

🎓 Development

Supporting future pilots, training programs and international aviation standards.
