About Georgian Paragliding Federation

We develop safe, professional and internationally recognized paragliding culture in Georgia. Our mission is to combine aviation safety, pilot education and unforgettable flying experiences.

🎯

Mission

Develop paragliding as a safe and structured aviation sport.

We create professional standards, promote safe flight operations and provide high quality tandem experiences for local and international visitors.
🛡

Safety

Safety is our highest priority in every flight.

We follow aviation safety principles, pilot qualification standards and operational procedures to ensure reliable and controlled flights.
🎓

Development

Building the future of Georgian paragliding.

We support pilot training, instructor development, competitions and cooperation with international aviation organizations.
